Our Story

A Cultural Bridge in the Heart of Adana

Founded by Fatoş, this gallery is not just a store—it is a labor of love and a sanctuary for those who appreciate true craftsmanship. For years, Fatoş Art & Gifts has proudly stood directly across from the Incirlik Air Base Main Gate, serving as a vibrant bridge between deep Anatolian heritage and art enthusiasts from all around the world.

Surrounded by our signature Turkish Blue walls, every single piece you find here has a soul and a story. From meticulously hand-woven kilims and warm mosaic lamps to our exclusive, custom-designed epoxy furniture, we ensure that you are not just taking home a souvenir, but a timeless piece of history.

We invite you to step inside, enjoy a cup of traditional Turkish tea, and discover the hidden treasures that transcend the ordinary.
